== Description ==

The Cafeteria Theme is the ideal choice for cafés, coffee shops, espresso bars, bistros, restaurants, bakeries, food service businesses, juice bars, fast food outlets, tea shops, ice cream parlors, pizza places, catering services, and sandwich shops looking to create a stylish and functional site with ease. Built with a powerful drag-and-drop page builder, this theme offers extensive customization options that allow you to design a website that reflects your brand’s unique identity. With its customizable layout, fonts, banners, and color schemes, you can match your café’s aesthetic without needing any coding skills. The theme comes with an engaging homepage slider to showcase featured products, daily specials, menus, and promotions with eye-catching visuals perfect for attracting attention instantly. For those looking to sell online, WooCommerce compatibility ensures seamless integration, making it ideal for café eCommerce, online ordering, bakery product sales, digital menu display, food delivery, catering booking, or takeaway services. Set up an online store to sell coffee beans, pastries, mugs, bakery items, brewing accessories, or merchandise directly from your site. Additionally, the dedicated food menu section lets you display your offerings in a clean, organized, and attractive format, making it easy for visitors to browse and explore. Whether you run a cozy café, a specialty coffee shop, a dessert shop, or a local bakery, the Cafeteria Theme provides all the essential features needed to build a modern, mobile-friendly, responsive, and SEO-optimized restaurant website that enhances your digital presence and boosts customer engagement.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your Cafeteria Elementor right away.
